# Adding Inventory Images

{% file src="../../../.gitbook/assets/inventory-images.zip" %}

<details>

<summary>For QB, PS, and LJ Inventory (and similar inventories)</summary>

Navigate to your inventory resource folder, then go to the <mark style="color:yellow;">`html`</mark> folder and place the images inside the <mark style="color:yellow;">`images`</mark> folder.

</details>

<details>

<summary>For OX Inventory</summary>

Navigate to your inventory resource folder, then go to the <mark style="color:yellow;">`web`</mark> folder and place the images inside the <mark style="color:yellow;">`images`</mark> folder.

</details>

<details>

<summary>For other inventory systems</summary>

Place the images in your inventory images folder. The exact path may vary depending on your setup.

</details>
